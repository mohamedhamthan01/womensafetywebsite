About

“I’m HERe” is a new app developed as a solution for women who want to stay safe when alone at night or even during the day.

When activated, the app sends information about your location to your trusted circle while capturing vital information in the background.

“I’m HERe” can be used anywhere: at home, school, work, and any outdoor activities.

Features

Track Me

The Track Me feature allows your loved ones to track you if you’re on a run or heading to the store.

This feature lets you select people with whom you’d want to share your real-time location and you can also choose how long you want to be tracked.

If you’re ever unable to notify your loved ones during an emergency and must be found, they’ll be able to locate you immediately.

Read More
SOS

SOS is designed to help you out of a dangerous situation. When activated, it sends out alerts to all your trusted contacts so that they can provide help or call for backup if needed.

SOS shares your location with your trusted contacts. It also captures photographs and records audio clips at equal intervals, which stay safe in your account for future evidence.

When the user is finally safe, the SOS function can be deactivated only after one of the trusted contacts inputs his unique safe code into the user’s app.

Help Line

The helpline feature is confidential and non-judgmental support staffed by trained professionals.

This feature has a fast response time and allows the user to receive advice and resources to help them feel safe again.

The helpline support is available 24/7 and provides comprehensive coverage for all types of emergencies.

Quick Tips

Quick tips provide quick and informative tips for a variety of emergency situations.

It allows the user to stay prepared, informed, and aware.

This feature lets the user feel confident in knowing what to do during an emergency.

It is a vital source of information that provides helpful advice to stay safe.
